about=about=This QGIS plugin was developed as part of the Master of Science in Water Sciences thesis entitled "Desarrollo de Complemento en QGIS para la Ubicación Óptima mediante PSO de Válvulas Reductoras de Presión en Redes de Distribución de Agua". The plugin integrates EPANET hydraulic models, WNTR simulations and Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO) techniques to support the optimal placement of Pressure Reducing Valves (PRVs) in water distribution networks. Acknowledgements: The author gratefully acknowledges the Master's Program in Water Sciences of the Universidad de Guanajuato for the academic and institutional support provided during this research, and SECIHTI Scholarship No. CVU 1344569 for supporting the completion of this work.
